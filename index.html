2048

Press the arrow buttons or keyboard keys to move all the tiles in that direction.
Each time you play a turn, a new tile is added.
If you play a turn and no tiles move, the game is finished.
If two of the same tiles are moved in a parallel direction, they will merge and the value will double.
Continue merging tiles until you reach 2048, then click that tile to make space on the grid
Keep a record of your score and see if you can beat your friends!
